~Welcome to The Legend of Zelda Remix~

Press 1 to toggle between godmode and normal mode.
Weapons acquired in godmode will carry over to normal mode, but collectables will not.

Press 4 to toggle between custom level and normal level.

Press H to switch to Bow
Press J to Switch to Bomb
Press K to Switch to Boomerang
Press L to Switch to Portal Gun (Only available in custom level)
	The Portal Gun can fire diagonally or in any of the WASD directions.
	However, it can only spawn portals on walls that have blocks in front of them!


Hope you enjoy!
